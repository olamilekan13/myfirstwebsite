This page
is an attempt to greet everyone on Earth using our various human languages. We're interested to see how long our list can become.

We'll start with languages that the Acedu staff could offer without using Google.

  • English: Hello, world!
  • Yoruba: E n le o, agbaye!
  • Japanese: Konnichiwa, sekai!
  • French: Bonjour, monde!
  • Kinyarwanda: Mwirwe, isi!
